Step 2: Taste and Slurp

Take a loud slurp! This spreads the coffee across your palate...

  • Sweetness: Natural sugars in the bean
  • Acidity: Like citrus or berries — not sourness
  • Body: The texture or weight of the coffee in your mouth
  • Flavor: Fruity, earthy, nutty, chocolaty?
