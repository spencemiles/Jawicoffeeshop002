Our Story

Brewing more than coffee — brewing community, love, and culture.

From a Single Bean to a Shared Dream

Jawi Coffee was born in the heart of Nairobi with a simple mission: to bring people together over a cup of locally sourced, perfectly brewed coffee. What started as a tiny corner shop with two passionate baristas has now grown into a cozy retreat known for its warmth, aroma, and authentic African blends.

Every bean tells a story. At Jawi, we work directly with small-scale farmers across East Africa to ensure that every roast supports sustainability and fair trade. Our team is made up of coffee lovers, artists, and dreamers — all working to create an experience you can feel in every sip.

We believe coffee isn’t just a drink — it’s a culture. A conversation starter. A daily ritual. And above all, a reason to slow down and savor life’s little moments.
